Facts About Child Hunger


According to latest FAO estimates in ‘The State of Food Insecurity in the World, 2015” report, 194.6 million people are undernourished in India.

Facts about hunger in India

  • India is home to the largest undernourished and hungry population in the world
  • 15.2% of our population is undernourished
  • 194.6million people go hungry everyday
  • 30.7% of children under 5 are underweight
  • 58% of children stunted by 2 years of age
  • 1 in 4 children malnourished
  • 3,000 children in India die every day from poor diet related illness
  • 24% of under-five deaths in India
  • 30% of neo-natal deaths in India

Why is the hunger in India so alarming?

The food grain production is touching new heights every year and we are producing more than sufficient to feed our people without relying on external support. However, sufficient food production is not the only criteria to provide food security in a country and eliminate hunger. Another critical factor that contributes to large scale hunger in India is the purchasing capacity of the households for nutritious and quality food. The households living below poverty line (BPL) do not have sufficient means to ensure food and nutritional security. NSSO data reveals that BPL households in rural India are spending as high as 70% of the consumption expenditure meeting their food requirements.

NEVER SLEEP HUNGRY

Skip 1 soda during your next meal , or skip 1 meal in your whole lifetime, 1 meal that is all what is needed.

One Meal of Your Life Can Bring Happiness to a Kid, dying of Hunger. One Meal from your entire LIFE that is all what we are asking you to give. You can spend money on burgers,pizza and soda but one meal in a day of your life if you skip it and donate it to someone in need you will be making someone in the world happier. Have you ever thought before you leave a piece of bread or a single grain in your plate and throw it, that tiny piece is the most thankful and important part of your life, what god has provided you with? To provide the families and individuals who are in need of this single grain/bread, we have created an international organization known as NEVERSLEEPHUNGRY, based in India and United States. Our Organization’s mission is to feed and distribute food and commodities to thousands of people who are living under poverty with no food and everyday necessities. We now have re-focused our energies on the extensive needs, primarily on hunger support in all parts of the world.

Our Mission

Currently, we started with a basic necessity of nutritious food and clean water. Our goal is to provide food, adequate clothing, supplies and education to needed communities around the world where these necessities are a luxury.

How It All Started?

Our founder Abhinav Pandey founded this neversleephungry project immediately after his coming to the United States of America. Abhinav came to USA for his education. Being from India when he came to United States of America he was astonished by seeing that there was a huge difference in lifestyle of people and the way they live and when he compared it to many small kids who sleep without food in many countries, He decided to make this a global effort. Abhinav knew there were many organization in India working on poor people but there is no organization providing free food. Small kids sleep without food for days. So Abhinav came up with an initiative to donate one meal of your life and by this he will help the kids not to sleep hungry in the streets. What he is asking for to citizens of these beautiful contries around the world to donate just one meal of your life. Abhinav is asking to skip a soda during your lunch , or skip the whole meal altogether and donate that money for providing food to a child who may be dying of hunger. "2 Million people can be fed each year by the amount of food thrown by a small country like Japan."
